About the Project

This interactive map was created as a complement to Duke University Theater Studies' 2017 production of Vinegar Tom. Vinegar Tom, written by Obie Award-winning playwright Caryl Churchill, tells the story of two sexually-spurned women in the 16th and 17th century England. The play examines the relationships between gender and power through the lens of witch trials; with this interactive, we hope to understand how witch trials have manifested in modern-day Europe. The content for this project was written by the dramaturg Meg Hancock, and Andrea Gonzales.
